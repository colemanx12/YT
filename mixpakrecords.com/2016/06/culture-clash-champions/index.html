June 24th, 2016

Culture Clash Champions!

Last weekend, Mixpak went to London to participate in the Red Bull Culture Clash and came home victorious! It was a spirited battle with Eskimo Dance, UKG All Stars and Wiz Khalifa & Taylor Gang, but we ultimately came out on top, bringing surprise guests including Big Narstie and dropping a huge exclusive dub of Drake’s “One Dance.” Check out some video below!
