Afro B x Vybz Kartel x Dre Skull
Shape Nice

“Shape Nice” is the new single from Afro B, Vybz Kartel and Dre Skull.

Bringing together the sounds & vocal styles of London, New York, Jamaica and Africa, the song is an encapsulation of the ever-present dialogue between afro-pop & dancehall.

Afro B is one of London’s rising stars, with his hit song “Drogba (Joanna)”, which took over London in 2018 and is now on playlist rotation on NY radio. Meanwhile, Jamaican artist Vybz Kartel continues to dominate dancehall, with an impressive career spanning decades and more hit songs than you can count. They are united by NY producer Dre Skull, known for his work with Popcaan, Kartel, Wizkid, Davido and more.
