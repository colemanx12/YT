Palmistry
Water

Palmistry is the project of London-based vocalist, songwriter and producer Benjy Keating, known for his stripped back pop and forward thinking production. “Water” is the first single from his sophomore album Afterlife, due May 2019.

Since the release of his debut, Pagan, he has been hunkered down in Athens, Greece & the Mixpak Studios in Brooklyn, NY making Afterlife. In the meantime, he has also had his work interpolated by Tory Lanez, Cashmere Cat & Major Lazer on “Miss You”, been tapped for production by Yung Lean, and made a full album for cult Vietnamese-Chinese artist Triad God.

“Water” is an intimate and impressionistic single produced by longtime collaborator SOPHIE, a woozy anthem about disconnection, and a taste of what’s to come
on Afterlife.
